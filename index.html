Our Philosophy

Craft elevated
to ceremony.

Onyx Atelier was born from a single conviction: that a manicure should never be rushed. In a world of conveyor-belt salons, we offer something rare — time, attention, and technique at the highest level.

Every client receives a private consultation. We study your hands, your lifestyle, your aesthetic. Then we create — meticulously, deliberately, with materials sourced from the finest houses in France, Italy, and Japan.

Isabelle Marton
Founder & Master Nail Artist

How It Works

The Onyx
Experience.

  • 1
    Private Consultation

    We begin with a conversation. Your nails, your lifestyle, your vision. We never assume — we listen. Your treatment is designed around you alone.

  • 2
    The Preparation Ritual

    Warm soak, cuticle refinement, shaping to your preferred architecture. Every hand is different; every shape is considered with care and intention.

  • 3
    Artistry & Finish

    Your chosen treatment applied in silence or conversation — as you prefer. We work with slow precision. Perfection takes the time it takes.

  • 4
    Aftercare Guidance

    Bespoke home-care recommendations, cuticle oils, and a follow-up window. We're invested in your results long after you leave our doors.
